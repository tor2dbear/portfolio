### Challenge

Lokalguiden identified an opportunity to serve newly opened businesses with a dedicated service. Nylokal needed its own brand identity to appeal to entrepreneurs and startup businesses while maintaining connection to Lokalguiden's trusted brand.

### Approach

**Brand Strategy**
- Positioned Nylokal as fresh, energetic, and startup-friendly
- Created visual identity that feels approachable and modern
- Balanced distinctiveness with Lokalguiden family resemblance

**Visual Identity**
- Developed logo and brand guidelines
- Created color palette and typography system
- Designed marketing materials and templates

### Solution

- **Energetic brand identity** appealing to new business owners
- **Flexible visual system** for various marketing applications
- **Clear brand guidelines** ensuring consistent implementation
- **Marketing materials** ready for campaign launches

### Impact

- Successful launch of new service line targeting emerging businesses
- Distinct identity helped differentiate offering in market
- Visual system enabled quick deployment of marketing campaigns
- Brand resonated with target entrepreneur audience

### Role

Art Director – Led brand identity development including logo design, visual guidelines, and marketing materials.
