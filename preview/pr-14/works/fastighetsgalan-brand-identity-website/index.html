Fastighetsgalan Brand Identity & Website – Sub-brand Development

Brand identity and website design for Fastighetsgalan, Lokalguiden's real estate industry awards event.

Monday, March 15, 2021
  • branding
  • web design
  • ui/ux

Challenge

Lokalguiden needed to launch Fastighetsgalan, an awards event targeting the real estate industry. The sub-brand required its own distinct identity while maintaining connection to the Lokalguiden parent brand. The project needed both visual identity and a functional event website.

Approach

Brand Strategy

  • Positioned Fastighetsgalan as prestigious and professional
  • Created visual identity that feels elevated while relating to Lokalguiden
  • Developed brand guidelines for consistent event materials

Website Design

  • Designed clean, professional website showcasing event information
  • Created clear user flows for nominations and registrations
  • Ensured mobile-responsive design for accessibility

Solution

  • Distinct brand identity that stands independently while complementing Lokalguiden
  • Professional event website with clear information architecture
  • Cohesive visual system applicable across digital and print materials
  • Registration/nomination flows designed for user ease

Impact

  • Successfully launched new event brand in competitive market
  • Professional presentation attracted high-quality participants
  • Website served as effective information hub and registration platform
  • Visual identity enhanced perceived value of awards

Role

Art Director – Created brand identity from concept to completion, designed and implemented website, established visual guidelines.
