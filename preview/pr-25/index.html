About Me

Product Owner & Art Director based in Gothenburg. I bridge design and product strategy – combining UI/UX expertise with data-driven prioritization.

Hi! My name is Torbjörn Hedberg. I lead digital product development by owning the backlog, writing user stories, and running sprints – while maintaining design quality through my background in visual communication, UI/UX design, and brand development.


My unique strength is understanding both what users need and what drives business value. I've evolved from Art Director to Product Owner, giving me the ability to not only design solutions, but also prioritize what should be built and why.

I lead development teams through structured, agile processes while ensuring every release is both functional and beautifully designed.

Working in 2-week sprints with developers and stakeholders, I translate business goals and user needs into concrete features. My day involves backlog management, sprint planning, user story writing, design system maintenance, and analyzing traffic and conversion metrics.


Whether you need someone who can drive product strategy, lead agile teams, or create exceptional user experiences – I bring all three.


Feel free to reach out if you'd like to connect!
