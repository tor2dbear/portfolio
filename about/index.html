Product designer based in Gothenburg. Specialized in graphic design with a focus on UI/UX.

Hi! My name is Torbjörn Hedberg. I am primarily involved in bringing ideas to life through user interfaces for web and mobile applications. I have a rich background in visual communication, working on projects involving print design, illustrations, and animations.


As a designer, I tackle problems at various levels, always aiming to create user-friendly products and solutions that are delightful.

Design thinking is an approach to problem solving – founded in the creative process.

I often collaborate closely with stakeholders and teams from diverse fields. This collaboration not only provides insight into the users' needs but also helps structure the team to create forward-thinking products.



Feel free to reach out if you'd like to connect!
