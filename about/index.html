Product designer based in Gothenburg. Specialized in graphic design with a focus on UI/UX.

Hi! My name is Torbjörn Hedberg. As of now, I’m primarily involved in the process of bringing ideas to realization in the form of user interfaces for the web and mobile applications. I do, however, have a rich background in the field of visual communication, working with projects in print design, illustrations and animations.


Through my work as a designer I tackle problems on different levels; always with the goal of creating a user friendly products and solutions that are joyfull.

Design thinking is an approach to problem solving – founded in the creative process.

I often work close together with steak holders and teams in a variety of fields. This allows me to, not only get insite into the needs of the user which is beeing designed for, but also to give structure to the team and to create products that has the future in mind.


When I’m not designing I spend time with my family, messing with front-end code, hanging out with friends – playing floorball or drinking a beer in one of Gothenburg’s pubs.


Feel free to reach out if you want to connect!
